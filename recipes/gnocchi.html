Sorrentine Gnocchi

Description

There are many varieties of gnocchi throughout Italy - from the sauce, toppings and overall flavor - it all depends on the region you find yourself in. For today, we are talking about the famous, Gnocchi alla Sorrentina, the specialty from Sorrento, Italy.

Ingredients

  • gnocchi
  • tomatoes
  • garlic
  • mozzarella
  • basil

Steps:

  1. For the sauce, saute the garlic in a little bit of olive oil in a large pan or skillet.
  2. Add the canned tomatoes with a pinch of salt and simmer for 10 minutes while breaking up the tomatoes enough so they bust open.
  3. Once simmered, add some mozzarella and basil and stir to combine.
  4. Now, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the gnocchi until they float to the surface.
  5. Remove the gnocchi and add to the tomatoes sauce, toss to coat.
  6. If your pan isn't ove-proof then transfer the gnocchi to an oven-proof baking dish.
  7. To bake, top the gnocchi with more mozzarella and bake for 20 minutes until the mozzarella is melted, bubbling and golden. And then you're done!
